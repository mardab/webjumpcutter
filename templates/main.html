WebJumpCutter
online frontend to video squeezer

Select video file to be processed (required):

Silence Threshold:    the volume amount that frames' audio needs to surpass to be consider ''sounded'' It ranges from 0 (silence) to 1 (max volume)

Speed of spoken frames:    the speed that sounded (spoken) frames should be played at. Typically 1.

Speed of silent frames:    the speed that silent frames should be played at. 999999 for full jumpcutting.

Context margin:    some silent frames adjacent to sounded frames are included to provide context. How many frames on either the side of speech should be included? That's this variable.
